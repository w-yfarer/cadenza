a half of a whole.

artemis and apollo are the greatest examples of solidarity amongst siblings in their family while also conceptualising the idea that the closest people can hurt you the most. while their siblings make weak alliances that crumble and change every week, the two of them can be seen frequently at each other's side until they're no longer equals and, well, it's always a sibling's responsibility to ground the other when they seem to be disconnected.

but when the two of them work together, they're a well-oiled machine. the reflection of the sun, there's a subtly of everything artemis does. a smile too sharp, a click of a butter knife as a warning over dinner, their overall goals are murky to everyone except, perhaps, apollo.
