Feral dogs that bite through the leash that once tied them, Hyperion is the consequence of absolute loyalty and obedience.

Most people either leave SpecOps via death, however there are those that grow out of their roles of soldiers or forced out of it – dropping completion rates, messy kills, you are not supposed to exist – and they retire, returning their OpTech gear and go back to living a civilian lifestyle.

But there are people who leave and disappear like a shadow in the night, using the very same gear they're supposed to return to be untraceable.

It starts with one headline and then another, and suddenly Kairos has a new rising group of criminals that use the same tech that is meant to get rid of them. They're making a name for themselves in the seedier areas of Kairos and rising among the ranks.

Basic Infomation • Most members of Hyperion are ex-Specter Operatives, however there are always exceptions and people have gained their attention before by being a general nusiance to Kairos' government.

• Due to the nature of the SpecOps, most memebers are heavily altered whether it be through ports or actual bionics. Non-SpecOp members tend to get their own version of OpTech gear.

• Their main base of operation is constantly changing, although it's currently a defunct metro stop in Sector Two - Lacuna.

• Makes most of their profit by being mercenaries for hire and through the illegal bionic trade. They help supply higher end bionics to shadier bionic technicians who won't ask how they got it.
